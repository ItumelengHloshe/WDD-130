Overview

Purpose

Want a way to stay up to date on your local Phoenix Suns basketball team? Ever wanted to find a page dedicated to your favorite sports team without the garbage editors at other worldwide sports sites? There is no need to look any further! Suns Late Night is dedicated to you and ensuring you are up to date on what is happening around the Phoenix Suns. Stay up to date on scores, highlights, and talks around the league involving the Phoenix Suns!

Audience

Phoenix Suns sports fans who want to keep in the know about the team are the audience for this page. They know the team well and its latest games, and want to see real-time information and interviews with players and coaches immediately as they happen. Old information can be accessed by this audience from the page, but itâ€™s not what they expect to see first. Rather they will be in the know about everything current with the Phoenix Suns and will use this page to keep as up to date as possible.

Typography

Heading Font: Righteous

Matches well with the Phoenix Suns logos and designs.

Paragraph Font: Lato, Helvetica, sans-serif

It's important that the paragraph fonts not be too busy, and easy to read, because that's where the majority of the content will be.

Content

Home page

Latest Suns Action Find out here the latest on the Suns playing--when, where, who, and the results. We will deliver you all the results and keep you in the know on what's coming up next with the Phoenix Suns as they go through their season. Look for great insights here to trends and players strengths and weaknesses as well as things to watch for from its players. When there are injuries, we'll tell you about them and the prospects for when players will return. When a player is hot, we'll make sure you see how the compare to other players around the league. You won't miss anything about the suns by coming to the page every day.

Know your Suns

Know your Suns Get informed about your favorite players history, stories, interests and more as you take a closer look to discover the men that make up your Phoenix Suns basketball team. Gain insights into who they are, their families, their upbringing, things that matter to to them off the court, and so much more. As you take a moment to get to know them better you'll feel more connected to their games and their emotions out on the court too. There is so much more to each one of these players and you can learn it all with a few clicks of the mouse. Join in a journey through your Suns players both past and present.

Join the Following

Join the Following You're not alone in wanting to keep up with all the live action with your Phoenix Suns. We have a network of fans all over the world that can come together and connect here receiving unique insights and updates on a regular basis. Plus get to know other fans and connet with them as you build up your Suns fandom over time.
